Who Are We?

We enjoy everything IoT, whether it be physical or digital. Through our meetings we hope to give live demonstrations, hands-on projects, and presentations about exciting topics to engage our community.

We are Student Integrated Technologies, a club dedicated to technology based subjects. During our meetings we cover topics such as general computer science, hardware, and software/coding.

We enjoy helping out others with their questions or issues, debugging and learning on the way. We hope to raise knowledge about technology on campus and in our community.


I'm Interested!

Do I need prior experience?

Nope! Anyone no matter their experience is welcome, we are open and ready to help anyone no matter their experience level. And don't worry, we hope to bring new knowledge to even those with prior knowledge.

Where can I find you?

We are currently located in room 543 (The computer lab / APCSP & AP CSA classroom) and we meet every Tuesday!*

*Unless specified otherwise

Do I need to sign up for the club?

Not at all! Anyone is welcome to join us for a minute or for the whole performance. Bring yourself or bring friends, it helps us grow as a community.

Do you have any socials?

Yes! You can contact us through our official Instagram account, Discord server, Gmail, or individually as linked below. We are also available during school hours and can be found around campus.

Are one-on-ones available?

Yes! We are available to tutor or simply touch up on topics if you need. We are always available for questions too, just say the word! You can also contact us through our Discord server.

How can I help?

There are many ways you could help. Most importantly, attending! We love to see our community grow, every bit counts! You can also refer us to your friends, classmates or peers.

    The Marketplace

    Our marketplace is somewhere where members of our community can sell, buy, or trade goods. Items such as computer parts are listed as we speak!


    Where Can I Buy/Sell Goods?

    1) You can speak to buyers / sellers during our meetings, before and after school*, or individually outside of school*.
    2) You can buy/sell items on our Discord servers official marketplace channel.
